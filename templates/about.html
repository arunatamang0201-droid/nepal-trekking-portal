About Nepal Trekking Portal

Welcome to Nepal Trekking Portal, your trusted partner for unforgettable Himalayan adventures.

Founded in 2020, we are a team of passionate trekkers and travel experts dedicated to providing authentic trekking experiences in Nepal. Our mission is to help adventurers from around the world discover the beauty of the Himalayas safely and responsibly.

Our Story

What started as a small group of local guides has grown into a comprehensive trekking platform serving hundreds of happy trekkers each year. We understand the mountains because we live in their shadow, and we share our knowledge and passion with every guest.
